About Bayangam

Bayangam (Yogam in the local language) is a district of Cameroon located in the Koung-Khi department of the West region in Bamileke country.
Bayangam was founded around the 16th century by Fohom Tchueng, who came from Baham with eight notables. The first chiefdom will be installed in the Tchala district and later moved to Hiala under the reign of Kom Mata, 8th Chief of the dynasty. In 2001, the village will induct its 13th chief

Source: mairiedebayangam.com

About Poo Yogam

“Poo Yogam”(Bayangam children in the local language) is a platform which aims to bring together Bayangam nationals around the world in order to promote actions of solidarity between them and the growth of projects for the development of Bayangam which is located in the department Koung-Khi, West region in Cameroon.
