FAQs

  • If I am already registered by a group, can I re-register individually or as a family?

    Indeed, you just have to ask the person in charge of your group to give you your unique code. From your email and your unique code, you can register in a family account or an individual account.

  • How do I submit a project or event?

    Send an email with the details of your project or event to enfantsbayangam@gmail.com and we will contact you.

  • I registered individually, can I add my family members later?

    Yes, you can add relatives at any time and your account will change to the family account.
